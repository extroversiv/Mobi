# The Mobi App for Garmin devices

Meet Mobi, the smart transit companion for your Garmin watch!
Mobi puts real-time train, tram, and bus departures right on your wrist, so you’re always one step ahead. 

Set up to four favorite stops for instant access, or let Mobi find the nearest departures based on your current location. Whether you’re sprinting for the train, heading home from a workout, or navigating a new city, Mobi keeps travel info simple, fast, and glanceable. No phone fumbling, no guesswork—just timely departures when you need them most. 

Commute smarter with Mobi!

The data is provided by transitious.org, you can find the covered regions here: https://transitous.org/sources/ and the privacy terms here: https://transitous.org/privacy/

This is a free app, but you can support further development through https://buymeacoffee.com/offi.garmin

Notes:
------
- You can change the preset stations either in the Garmin Connect IQ Store or in the Garmin Connect app on your phone, see https://support.garmin.com/en-US/?faq=NKbZf5f3kp1XGwWRNmSOU9
- Departure times without realtime have a '¦' after the time,  realtime departures have a '|' after the time.
- You can filter the types of transportation by adding single capital characters separated by '+' in front of the station name, e.g. 'S+U+R Berlin Hauptbahnhof' would only show S-Bahn, U-Bahn and regional train departures at the station Hauptbahnhof in Berlin. Available filters are:
  - X - express trains
  - R - regional and local trains
  - S - suburban rail
  - U - subway, metro, underground
  - B - bus
  - C - coach
  - F - ferry
- Pressing the menu-button clears the cache to reset the assigned preset stops.
